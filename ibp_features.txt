IBP — Identity-Based Profiler
Unified OSINT Investigation Platform
=====================================

IBP is a professional background check and open-source intelligence platform
designed for investigating individuals using publicly available data sources.
Enter a Russian Tax ID (INN) and IBP runs a fully automated 9-stage investigation
pipeline, delivering a comprehensive dossier in minutes.


HOW IT WORKS
============

You provide a person's INN (Russian Tax ID). IBP verifies it, confirms the
identity through official government records, and then automatically runs
through 9 investigation stages — from government registries to social media
to behavioral analysis — building a complete profile.

Two modes are available:
  • Quick Mode — runs all stages automatically, delivers results fast.
  • Precise Mode — pauses after social media discovery so you can confirm
    the correct profiles before continuing. Higher accuracy, slightly longer.


THE 9 STAGES
=============

STAGE 0 — Identity Confirmation
  What it does: Verifies the person's identity using their tax ID.
  How: Looks up the INN in official business registries, checks for bankruptcy
  filings, maps out any business connections, and confirms the full legal name.
  This confirmed name is used by every stage that follows.

STAGE 1 — Government Registries
  What it does: Searches official government databases.
  How: Checks business registration records, court case databases, and
  enforcement proceedings (debts, fines, unpaid obligations). Covers both
  civil and arbitration courts.

STAGE 2 — Security Checks
  What it does: Screens against security and sanctions databases.
  How: Checks global sanctions lists (including Russian, US, EU, and UN lists),
  wanted persons databases, Interpol notices, and extremist watchlists.

STAGE 3 — Social Media Discovery
  What it does: Finds the person's social media profiles.
  How: Searches VK (Russia's largest social network), Telegram, and
  Telegram using multiple search strategies. Filters results by date
  of birth and other identifiers to find the right person. In Precise Mode,
  you review and confirm profiles before proceeding.

STAGE 4 — Contact Discovery
  What it does: Finds phone numbers, email addresses, and other contact info.
  How: Extracts contacts from social media profiles, business records, and
  public posts. Generates possible email addresses based on naming patterns.
  Cross-references findings across breach databases and public data leaks.
  Checks marketplace listings (Avito, CIAN, Auto.ru, and others) for
  associated phone numbers. Verifies which email services the person uses.
  Every contact gets a confidence score based on how many independent sources
  confirmed it.

STAGE 5 — Deep Social Analysis
  What it does: Expands the investigation using advanced techniques.
  How: Runs facial recognition searches across photo databases. Builds a
  social graph showing the person's network of friends and connections,
  identifying key clusters and influential contacts. Searches thousands of
  websites for matching usernames. Any new accounts found loop back into
  Stage 4 for additional contact extraction.

STAGE 6 — Behavioral Intelligence
  What it does: Analyzes the person's online behavior and patterns.
  How: Examines public posts for sentiment, topics, and keywords. Extracts
  geographic data — cities mentioned, locations tagged, areas of activity.
  Builds an activity timeline showing when and where the person is most
  active online.

STAGE 7 — Risk Scoring
  What it does: Calculates a comprehensive risk assessment.
  How: Scores the person across 9 risk categories including financial risk,
  legal exposure, security flags, social behavior, and identity verification.
  Produces an overall rating: CLEAN, LOW, MEDIUM, HIGH, or CRITICAL.

STAGE 8 — Report Generation
  What it does: Compiles everything into a professional dossier.
  How: Generates a structured report with an identity card, all findings
  organized by stage, a social network graph, geographic activity map,
  and risk breakdown. Available as an interactive web page, downloadable
  PDF, or raw JSON data.

CONFIDENCE SCORING
==================

Every piece of information IBP finds comes with a confidence score from 0 to 1.
The more independent sources confirm the same data point, the higher the score.
When 3 or more sources agree, the score gets an automatic boost.

  High confidence (0.75+)  — verified by multiple reliable sources
  Medium confidence (0.50+) — found in at least one credible source
  Low confidence (below 0.50) — single source or pattern-based estimate


DATA SOURCES
============

IBP aggregates data from a wide range of publicly available sources:

  • Official government registries and APIs
  • Court and legal databases
  • International sanctions and watchlists
  • Social media platforms (VK, Telegram)
  • Public breach and leak databases
  • Online marketplaces and classifieds
  • Username and facial recognition search engines
  • Email verification services
  • Public business records

All data is collected from publicly accessible sources. No private databases
are accessed. No unauthorized access is performed.
