About

Unified OSINT
Investigation Platform

СЛЕД is an advanced background check system designed for authorized investigators. It automates the collection and cross-referencing of data from government registries, social networks, breach databases, and behavioral signals into a single comprehensive dossier.

INN-First Architecture

Russian Tax ID is the primary identifier, enabling direct registry lookups before any name-based search.

9-Stage Automated Pipeline

From identity confirmation to PDF dossier — fully automated with real-time progress tracking.

Quick & Precise Modes

Quick mode runs all stages unattended. Precise mode pauses after social discovery for profile confirmation.
